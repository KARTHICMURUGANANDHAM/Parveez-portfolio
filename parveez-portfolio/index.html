About Me

Engineering thoughtful products, one detail at a time.

I'm a final-year Computer Science & Engineering student at Nandha Engineering College, Erode, graduating in 2026 with a CGPA of 8.2. My work sits at the intersection of software engineering and design — I like building systems that are technically solid and genuinely pleasant to use.

My interest in technology started with curiosity about how things connect — from wiring up an IoT-based car automation prototype to designing an AI-powered fare system that recognises commuters through geolocation and face verification. Along the way I picked up React and Figma, and started paying as much attention to how an interface feels as to how it functions.

In early 2025 I interned as a Full Stack Web Development intern at Accent Techno Soft in Coimbatore, where I built features with React and Node.js and worked directly with APIs and databases in a production setting. Outside of coursework, I've represented my college at the AR/VR workshop Yugam-2023 and competed at Techsurf and the CIT Hackathon 2024.

What motivates me is simple: I want to ship things people actually enjoy using. Whether that's a fare-collection system for a city bus, or a travel planner that removes friction from a decision — the goal is always usability, clarity, and a bit of delight.
